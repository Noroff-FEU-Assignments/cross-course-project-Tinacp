ABOUT US

Rainy Days founded in January 2022 by James Olsson and Gordon Smith, with a dream of producing high-tech rain and explorer jackets to a lower price range.

Our extremely waterproof and breathable rain jackets protect you on the wettest days. All our rain gear features our innovative weather protection technology, designed with input from professionals who need a heavy-duty shield from the elements. No other manufacturer of outdoor equipment can match our experience in polar exploration or our ambition to share this legacy with todays adventurers.
